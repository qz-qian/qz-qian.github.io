About

Zhe Qian

Welcome to my homepage! Currently I'm a third-year undergraduate student at South China Agricultural University, majoring in Robotics Engineering. I'm a curious, self-motivated student who loves tackling meaningful problems. My favorite fictional character is Iron Man, and I firmly believe his J.A.R.V.I.S. system will one day become reality.

My research interests include AI4Science, Computer Vision, Language Modeling, Reinforcement Learning, and Agentic Systems. I'm lucky to be surrounded by amazing mentors and peers. To me, being a scientist means being both a researcher and an engineer, and I strive to live out this philosophy with courage and purpose.
